Success Story: Jane's Journey

Posted on November 1, 2024 by SMEEF

“SMEEF helped me gain skills that allowed me to start my own business and provide for my family. Before joining SMEEF, I struggled to make ends meet. The skills training I received enabled me to open a small craft store, and now I can support my children and plan for their future.”

Jane's story is just one of many inspiring journeys we see at SMEEF. Our programs are designed to uplift and empower single mothers in Rwanda, helping them become self-sufficient and successful entrepreneurs.

Above: A glimpse of Jane's craft store, where she sells handmade products created during her training.
