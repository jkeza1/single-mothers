About Us

SMEEF (Single Mothers Economic Empowerment Framework) is committed to empowering single mothers in Rwanda. We offer comprehensive training in entrepreneurship, life skills, and business development, enabling them to become financially independent and create a positive future for their children.

Our approach is based on mentorship, community support, and sustainable business practices that provide real, long-term solutions for single mothers.

Empowering Through Skills Development

At SMEEF, we offer programs designed to help single mothers gain self-sufficiency by building valuable skills that unlock new opportunities. Each program is tailored to provide practical, hands-on experience in a supportive environment.
