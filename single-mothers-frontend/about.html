About Us

At SMEEF, we are dedicated to empowering single mothers in Rwanda by offering comprehensive training in entrepreneurship, life skills, and business development. Our mission is to help them become financially independent and build a better future for their families.

Through mentorship, community support, and sustainable business practices, we create lasting, positive change in the lives of single mothers.
